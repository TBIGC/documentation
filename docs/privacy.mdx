TBI takes the privacy of its clients data seriously. We do not share any data
either internally or with 3rd parties. We do not do analysis on the data hosted
within the dashboard software, nor do we compare data between different dashboards
and governments.

For more details please read our privacy policy [here](https://institute.global/institute/privacy-policy)
